BOWNE & CO., STATIONERS

GRAPHIC DESIGN | IDENTITY | PRINTING

Our interest in New York City history led to a fascinating ongoing relationship with Bowne & Co. Stationers, a re-creation of a 19th-century job printing shop that is part of the South Street Seaport Museum. Bowne uses antique type and treadle presses to produce products sold in the shop. We developed a new logo for Bowne and designed and printed dozens of cards and posters incorporating items we found in the museum’s collections.
