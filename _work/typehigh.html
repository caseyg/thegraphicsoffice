TYPEHIGH

GRAPHIC DESIGN | IDENTITY | PRINTING

TypeHigh is a line of greeting cards and ephemera we developed that mixed 19th-century ornament and contemporary sentiment. We sold in stores and on Etsy, and the brand was even featured in Stephen Heller’s book Design Entrepreneur. We printed the cards ourselves on antique presses; it was labor intensive but extremely satisfying.
