SHELLEY’S GHOST

GRAPHIC DESIGN | WEB DESIGN

This exhibition of the Romantic poet Percy Bysshe Shelley brought together materials from the New York Public Library and the Bodleian Library in Oxford, England. In addition to our collaboration with Barbara Suhr on all aspects of installation design, we created print materials, signage and letterpress-printed mementoes. In addition, we proposed a curated digital “readers shelf” of Shelley’s works. [imagelist]
