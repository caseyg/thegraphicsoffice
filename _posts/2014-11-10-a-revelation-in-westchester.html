Hope Cemetery in Hastings-on-Hudson, NY, which is a short drive from Manhattan into Westchester County. The Jewish parts of the ethnically diverse cemetery held some pleasant surprises; we found sleek designs that make granite look surprisingly good, as well as traditional symbols, forms, and placement that are new to me. Finally, some well-designed, modern gravestones! The crisp afternoon light and fall foliage enhanced the scene. Following are some of our best photographs.
